REGULATIONS

  1. This regulation states the rights and duties of atenees of the Snepcon 2020 convention, which takes place from 26.02.2020 - 01.03.2020.
  2. Each and every one of the convention attendees of the Snepcon, organized by MizuLynx is obliged to read the regulations.
  3. By registering at the convention website, the attendee agrees to terms of these regulations.
  4. Both Snepcon and the hotel regulations will be available during the convention at the hotel entrance as well as in electronic form.
  5. Those who will not obey the regulations may be removed from the convention without the right to have their money back.
  6. Organizer reserves the right to have the final decision regarding regulations interpretation.
  7. In matters not mentioned in the regulations, the provisions of the Civil Code shall be applied.
